SOUND FILES GO HERE

This project requires the following sound files to be placed in this directory:

1. success.wav - Success sound (default)
2. warning.wav - Warning sound
3. error.wav - Error sound

You can download free notification sounds from:
- https://freesound.org
- https://mixkit.co/free-sound-effects/notification
- https://notificationsounds.com

Please ensure all sound files are free for commercial use and have appropriate licensing.
